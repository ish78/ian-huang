About me

My name is Ian Huang. I graduated from USC with a major in Architectural Studies and a minor in Ceramics. I like working with my hands and making things with a variety of materials and methods.

I'm always collecting new ideas, techniques, and scavenged materials for potential projects. When I'm not creating things, you can find me playing tennis, volleyball, bouldering, or zooming around on my Onewheel.
